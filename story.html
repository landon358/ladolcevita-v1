Our Story

Petoskey’s
sweet life.

A boutique salon in the heart of a resort community, built on craft, warmth, and the belief that every head of hair deserves unhurried attention.

The first choice of locals and seasonal residents alike, La Dolce Vita has been Petoskey's home for expert hair for years.

We built La Dolce Vita around a simple idea, that a salon visit should feel like a small luxury rather than an errand. No rushing, no assembly line. Just a stylist giving you their full attention, from the first consultation to the final mirror.

Our team specializes in the work other salons shy away from. Curly and textured hair, precision short cuts, men's styling, and color correction that takes patience and a trained eye. All hair types and textures are genuinely welcome here, and celebrated.

We finish every service with the finest professional lines in the world, so the results you love in the chair are results you can recreate at home.
